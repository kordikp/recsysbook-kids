About the author

Pavel Kordik is co-founder of Recombee, a recommendation engine used by hundreds of services worldwide, and associate professor at Czech Technical University.

"One day my daughter asked me: Dad, how does YouTube know what I want to watch? I realized I'd spent 15 years building recommendation systems but never explained them simply. This book is my answer — for her, and for every curious kid who wants to understand the technology that shapes what they see online."
