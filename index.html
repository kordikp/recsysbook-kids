How Recommendations Work

Understand the algorithms that decide what you see online

Pavel Kordik — about the author

I co-founded Recombee, a recommendation engine that powers thousands of services worldwide. We've spent over 15 years building systems that decide what people see on their screens.

We know how powerful this technology is — and how important it is to understand it. This book shares what we've learned: how recommendations work, why they matter, and what to watch out for to avoid the negative consequences.

Whether you want to build recommendation systems or just be a smarter user of the apps you use every day — this book is for you.
